About Us

DAVMAA was started with the vision to bring together the love of DAVians for their Alma mater, direct their energies in a common direction so that their efforts can culminate into something larger than themselves and that lives longer than any of us.

It is a platform that would help connect alumni from different phase of lives to their beloved teachers and also to the current students of the school. It also provides an effective means of networking for alumni and students.

It will work to improve the plight of the current students by providing them with exposure of the current global scenario in various different fields ranging from Technology, Medicine to Sports and Music.

Build on strong values, the association would strive to keep alive the spiritual knowledge that was instilled in each one of us during our time at DAV.

Objectives

• To provide a forum to establish a link between the alumni, staff and students of the school.

• To enable the alumni to participate in activities which would contribute to the general development of the school.

• To award scholarships and render financial aid to deserving students of the School.

• To contribute towards the welfare of the alumni.

Functions

• Communication (newsletters, mailing, magazines, etc)
• Organizing programs and workshops for students overall development.
• Fund raising events
• Scholarships, fellowships and awards for deserving students
• Identify short and long term needs of the school
• Social activities like funding the education of orphan(s)
• Sponsoring events organized by the school
• Annual interaction session with students for ‘career guidance’
